<details>
<summary>My Deluxe settings are not saving.</summary>
You set "**Confirm DX Settings Save**" to **Off**.  
You can enable it again in:  
`Menu > Options > Deluxe Settings > Advanced > Functional Options > Confirm DX Settings Save`
</details>

<details>
<summary>I ruined the track camera settings.</summary>
Restarting your game will reset them back to normal.
</details>
