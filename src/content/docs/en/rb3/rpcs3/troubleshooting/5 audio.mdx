<details>
<summary>There's noticable latency, especially on vocals.</summary>
* Lower the **Audio Buffer Duration** slider in the [[Audio tab when setting up a Custom Configuration]](https://rb3pc.milohax.org/custom_config_aud) for Rock Band 3.
* Deactivate pitch correction in Rock Band 3.
    * To do this, go to `Menu > Options > Vocal Options > Pitch Correction`
* Disable Dolby Digital.
* Connect straight to your sound system and avoid using your TV for audio or HDMI ARC.
</details>
